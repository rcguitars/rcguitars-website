About

I’ve been a musician for as long as I can remember, picking up various different instruments until I landed on the guitar/vocals combo in my teens. I’ve played in various bands and have worked with incredible musicians over the years.

The most important thing I’ve learned in my life is that everyone has a unique way of understanding the world. I endeavour to reflect this in my teaching method and to work together with you to find the right path for your learning.

I’ve worked with children and young people since 2011 with my partner, doing music and movement workshops.

Learning works best when the student is fully engaged. The methods I use for teaching reflect the students’ needs, with an adaptive (holistic?) approach centred around having fun.

Learning guitar can be challenging and frustrating, so by making the milestones achievable and enjoyable, progress comes easily.
